Set up the integral to compute the length of the helix corresponding to 5 complete turns, and evaluate it by hand.

\begin{solution}
\begin{align*}
    L &= \int_{0}^{10\pi} |\boldsymbol{r}'(u)| du \\
    &= \int_{0}^{10\pi} \sqrt{a^2+b^2}du \\
    &= 10\pi \sqrt{a^2+b^2}
\end{align*}
\end{solution}
