Plot the position of the center of mass for the range $0 \leq \phi \leq 4\pi$. 

\begin{solution} \
\begin{lstlisting}
phi = [0:0.1:4*pi];
x_cg = r * phi - (r - ybar)*sin(phi);
y_cg = r - (r - ybar)*cos(phi);
plot(x_cg, y_cg, '.')
axis equal
\end{lstlisting}
