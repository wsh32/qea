What would happen if you used this matrix for $a$ instead:

\begin{lstlisting}[language=Matlab]
a = [(1:10)',(1:10)'/2 -(1:10)']
\end{lstlisting}

\begin{solution}
    Because the numbers are negatively correlated, the correlation would be:
    
    \begin{align*}
        \boldsymbol{C} = \begin{bmatrix}
            1 & -1 \\
            -1 & 1
        \end{bmatrix}
    \end{align*}
\end{solution}
