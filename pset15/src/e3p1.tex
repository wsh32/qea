Classify the critical points of $f(x)$

\begin{solution}
\begin{align*}
    f''(x) = 12x^2 - 2 \\
    f''(0) = -2 \\
    f''\left(\pm\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\right) &= 4 \\
\end{align*}

The critical point $x=0$ is a local maximum and the critical points at $x=\pm\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}$ are local minimums.
\end{solution}
