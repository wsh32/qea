Determine the gradient field defined by $\nabla V$ , and visualize it.

\begin{solution}
\begin{align*}
    \nabla V &= \begin{bmatrix} \frac{\partial V}{\partial x} \\ \frac{\partial V}{\partial y} \end{bmatrix} \\
    &= \begin{bmatrix}
        \frac{x}{x^2+y^2} \\
        \frac{y}{x^2+y^2}
    \end{bmatrix}
\end{align*}
