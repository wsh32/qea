How would you generalize translation to 3D?

\begin{solution}
    The 3D vector matrix should have an extra row, making it:
    
    \begin{align*}
        \begin{bmatrix}
            x \\ y \\ z \\ 1
        \end{bmatrix}
    \end{align*}
    
    The 3D transformation matrix is:
    
    \begin{align*}
        \boldsymbol{T} = \begin{bmatrix}
            1 & 0 & 0 & t_x \\
            0 & 1 & 0 & t_y \\
            0 & 0 & 1 & t_z \\
            0 & 0 & 0 & 1
        \end{bmatrix}
    \end{align*}
\end{solution}
