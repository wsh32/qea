Show that $\boldsymbol{Tv}$ accomplishes the process of translation. What is the final vector?

\begin{solution}
\begin{align*}
    \boldsymbol{v} = \begin{bmatrix}
        x \\ y
    \end{bmatrix} \\
    \boldsymbol{T} = \begin{bmatrix}
        1 & 0 & t_x \\
        0 & 1 & t_y \\
        0 & 0 & 1
    \end{bmatrix} \\
    \boldsymbol{Tv} = \begin{bmatrix}
        x + t_x \\ y + t_y \\ 1 
    \end{bmatrix}
\end{align*}
\end{solution}
