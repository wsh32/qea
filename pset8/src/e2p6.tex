How long does it take an initial population of $\frac{A}{2}$ to reach 99\% of the carrying capacity if $k = 0.1$

\begin{solution}
    \begin{align*}
        0.99A &= \frac{A}{1 + e^{-0.1t}} \\
        e^{-0.1T} &= \frac{0.01}{0.99} \\
        -0.01T &= \ln{\frac{0.01}{0.99}} \\
        T &\approx 46
    \end{align*}
\end{solution}
