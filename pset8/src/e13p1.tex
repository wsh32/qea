Visualize the elliptic paraboloid $z = x^2/a^2 + y^2/b^2$ for different values of $a > 0$ and $b > 0$.

\begin{solution}\
\begin{lstlisting}
[x, y] = meshgrid(linspace(-10, 10, 100), linspace(-10, 10, 100));
z = x.^2/a^2 + y.^2/b^2;
surf(x, y, z);
shading interp
\end{lstlisting}
