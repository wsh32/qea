What are the equations for the left and right wheel velocities?

\begin{solution}
\begin{align*}
    V_L &= V - \omega\frac{d}{2} \\
    V_R &= V + \omega\frac{d}{2}
\end{align*}

syms d

V_R = simplify(V_T + w(3) * d / 2)
