Evaluate $\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}$ and $\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}$ for each of the following functions.

\begin{align*}
    f(x, y) = x^2\sin(xy^2)
\end{align*}

\begin{solution}
\begin{align*}
    \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} &= 2x\sin(xy^2) + x^2y^2 \cos(xy^2) \\
    \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} &= 2x^3y\cos(xy^2) \\
\end{align*}
\end{solution}
