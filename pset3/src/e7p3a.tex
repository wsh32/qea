Formulate a matrix-vector equation to find out the numbers of oranges, apples and pears you have.

\begin{solution}
    \begin{align*}
        \begin{bmatrix}
            14 \\ 45 \\ 21
        \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}
            1 & 1 & 1 \\
            4 & 3 & 3 \\
            2 & 1 & 3
        \end{bmatrix} * \begin{bmatrix}
            n_o \\ n_a \\ n_p
        \end{bmatrix}
    \end{align*}
\end{solution}
