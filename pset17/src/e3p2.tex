With reference to Figures 42.5 and 42.6, express the location of the object with respect to the NEATO frame N.

\begin{solution}
\begin{align*}
    r_N &= r_{NL} r_L \\
    r_{NL} &= \begin{bmatrix}
        1 & 0 & -d \\
        0 & 1 & 0 \\
        0 & 0 & 1
    \end{bmatrix} \\
    r_N &= \begin{bmatrix}
        r\cos\theta - d \\
        r\sin\theta
    \end{bmatrix}
\end{align*}
\end{solution}
